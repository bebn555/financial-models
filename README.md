# 📊 NVIDIA vs AMD — Equity Research & DCF Valuation Model

**Semiconductor Sector | Equity Research Project**  
*Applied concepts from Yale University — Financial Markets (Prof. Robert Shiller, Coursera)*

## 📁 Model Overview
A comprehensive 7-sheet financial model independently built to analyze and compare NVIDIA (NVDA) and AMD across three frameworks:

- ✅ **3-Statement Financial Model** — Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow (FY2022–FY2026E)
- ✅ **Cost Structure & Profitability Analysis** — Margin breakdown and head-to-head comparison
- ✅ **DCF Valuation** — Discounted Cash Flow with adjustable assumptions
- ✅ **Comparable Company Analysis** — EV/Revenue, EV/EBITDA, P/E across 7 semiconductor peers

## 📌 Data Sources
- SEC EDGAR 10-K filings (FY2022–FY2025A)
- Analyst consensus estimates (FY2026E)
